FARNSWORTH MODEL

I have always been drawn to modern architecture, especially the work of Mies van der Rhoe. The Farnsworth House might be my favorite. Beautiful in its simplicity and harmonious structure--I love how it opens up to its setting in nature. I constructed a scale model out of corregated cardboard and brought it to Mies' buildings on Lake Shore Drive in downtown Chicago. I was interested in how my image of the model changed in the varous settings I took it to.
