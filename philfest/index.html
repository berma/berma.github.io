PHILFEST CAMPAIGN

Philfest is Northwestern University's annual bluegrass festival which benefitts the Rocky Mountain Institute, a nonprofit that promotes renewable energy. The environmental group organizing the event wanted to avoid advertising with paper fliers.

To do this I cut out a stencil from a piece of acetate and scraped mud through it in various locations around campus. The mud dries and remains visible in the concrete until the next rainstorm.
